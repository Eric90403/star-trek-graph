TEASER

FADE IN:

Captain's log, stardate 47623.2. The Enterprise is conducting a routine astronomical survey of the Denara Sector when we received an automated distress signal—faint, ancient, and originating from a vessel that appears to have been adrift for centuries. Initial scans suggest technology far beyond anything in our database. Commander Data estimates the signal has been repeating for over two hundred years, which raises a troubling question: if anyone was alive to send it, why has no one answered? We are moving to intercept. I cannot shake the feeling that we are about to encounter something unprecedented—and that whatever we find will demand more from us than simple First Contact protocols can provide.

FADE OUT.

On screen.

RIKER

PICARD

Every moment. Every endless, silent moment.

**ACT II**

And?

PICARD
I have not yet received his report.

INT. ENGINEERING LAB - DAY

(The engineering lab has been transformed into a careful surgical space for digital entities.
